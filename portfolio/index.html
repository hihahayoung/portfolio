  • Econometrics Research: A Synthetic Control Study on Police Killings in the US

    Fall 2024
    Academic
    Evaluated the impact of state-level body camera mandates on police-related fatalities using the synthetic control method. Focusing on New Jersey and New Mexico as treatment states, I constructed counterfactuals from a donor pool of 43 control states to isolate the effect of body-worn camera (BWC) policies. Drawing from multiple datasets—including Mapping Police Violence, FBI crime data, and U.S. Census statistics—I modeled how demographic, political, and crime-related variables influence police violence.
